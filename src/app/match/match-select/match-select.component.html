Practical information

When you click "reuse" Boklisten buys your item and you will not need to pay the second payment. You will be accountable to deliver the item to another customer or to Boklisten if no customer is matched.

You will get an SMS about meeting time and place. You can decide to opt out on a later time. If you opt out you must pay the second payment for your items.

More information about the "Reuse" incentive can be read at our info page
