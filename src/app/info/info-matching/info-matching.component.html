Matching

Information about Boklistens concept of matching
What happens next semester when I need to deliver my items?

If you choose that your book shall be reused you will get a message in January about meeting time and place and who you will deliver to.

How do I get payed by Boklisten?

When reciever confirms that he or she has gotten the items you will get 50 NOK per item sent to you by VIPS. When you select "reuse" you sell the item to Boklisten for the same amount as your outstanding payment.

How will I know who to deliver the items to?

You will get an SMS with phone number and name of the reciever and the reciever will get the same from you.

How and when will the items be handed over?

Items shall be handed over on your school inside working hours. You will get SMS about exact time and date.

What happes when the meeting time does not suit me?

If the meeting time does not fit with your schedule you will need to notify the reciever and instead deliver the items to Boklisten.

What if I want to keep the items instead?

If you want to keep the items you must pay the original outstanding amount.

What if I no longer have the item?

If you no longer have the item you must notify Boklisten and pay the outstanding amount.

What happens if no one needs one or more of my items next semester?

You will get info on how to deliver them to our stand at Boklisten.

What is the upside of participate in this?

When you participate you help to reuse and recycle items. You also will be sure that Boklisten pays for your second payment.
