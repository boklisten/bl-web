Frequently asked questions
How do I rent books as a private candidate?

You can either order some books in the webstore and then get it at a branch, or you could just meet up at our branch at our opening times. The opening times will be added at the webpage in good time for the semester end.

How long does it take to get the items?

If you order by mail it will take up to 14 days to send the delivery to you. If you want to get the items at a branch you can get them at once. (It can take longer if we don't have that item in store, as we need to order some more)

Can I buy items at Boklisten.no?

Yes, but we really want you to consider to rent instead, it's cheaper and it's more environmental friendly. You don't need the items after you have passed the exams, do you?

Do I have to order online?

As a regular student you need to order online. As a private candidate you can choose to either order online or come to one of our branches to order there. We strongly suggest that you order online as it will take you shorter time to get the items once you are at the branch.

Where do I deliver the items?

As a regular student you deliver them at the same time as the rest of your class. As a private candidate you deliver the items at the branch you ordered them from. If you can't deliver them at our opening times you can deliver them by mail.

Can someone else deliver for me?

Yes, you can have someone else deliver the items for you. But you the one who deliver should bring your id.

What do I do if i'm not satisfied with one of the items?

If you deliver it back to us you can get a new one. If you want your money back or just want to return the item please read our complaint and cancellation page.

Can I keep the items?

Yes, but if you don't deliver the books in set time you will get a replacement invoice. This invoice is the items full price + 10% + 90 kr in fee. This will become expensive and we recomend you to deliver in time! If you are a private candidate you can buyout the item in the webstore under the page "Your items" (before the deadline of course)

Can I extend the deadline?

If you are a private candidate you can extend the deadline up to one semester each. Beware however, you need to do this before the old deadline!

What do I do if the deadline is expired?

You will get notified about what to do, either by email, sms or both. The general rule is that you will receive an invoice which you have to pay. You can read more about this in our rent agreement.

Can I mark in the books?

Yes, but we recommend you not to mark with pen or marker, only with pencils.
