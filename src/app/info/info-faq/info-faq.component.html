Frequently asked questions
How do I rent books as a private candidate?

You can either order some books in the webstore and then get it at a branch, or you could just meet up at our branch at our opening times. The opening times will be added at the webpage in good time for the semester end.

Can I buy books at Boklisten.no?

Yes, but we really want you to consider to rent instead, don't you want to save the planet?

Can I keep the books?

Yes, but if you don't deliver the books in set time you will get a replacement invoice. This invoice is the books full price + 10% + 90 kr in fee. This will become expensive and we recomend you to deliver in time! If you are a private candidate you can buyout the book in the webstore under the page "Your items" (before the deadline of course)

Can I extend the deadline?

If you are a private candidate you can extend the deadline up to one semester each. Beware however, you need to do this before the old deadline!

Can I mark in the books?

Yes, but we recomend you not to mark with pen or marker, only with pencils.
